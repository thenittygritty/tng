> It doesn’t matter how many features you have or how sexy your features are if they aren’t delivered to the user quickly, with ease, and then heavily monitored. Front-end operations engineers are the **enablers of long-term progress**.

Word.

I absolutely love this article. Alex compiled a very thorough list of tasks that could fall into the responsibility of a so called front-end operations engineer. If you are going to implement such a role in your company or not and if that makes sense is very dependable on what you are actually working on. But at the end of the day all front-end engineers should be aware of these tasks and prioritize and enforce their execution as needed.
